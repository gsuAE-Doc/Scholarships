Other Resources

There are many web-based resources at Georgia State University. This page highlights some of particular interest to students preparing for the scholarship process. We encourage you to use these additional resources to enhance your competitiveness, discover additional funding opportunities and determine how much you will need. 

All scholarship applicants, including HOPE recipients, must reapply for financial aid each year. The Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) must be completed in order to qualify for Georgia State University scholarships and many external awards. You can access the FAFSA at www.fafsa.ed.gov and the GSFAPP at http://gacollege411.org/.   

  • Student Success
    Here you will find information on our advisement process, Freshmen Learning Communities, Supplemental Instruction, Academic Coaching, and much more.
  • PAWS
    Current and former Georgia State University students may order transcripts via PAWS. You can pick up your transcript at 227 Sparks Hall (photo ID required) or have it mailed to you. 
  • Disability Services
    Provides accommodations including specific testing, scholarships and parking needs for students with disabilities.
  • Financial Aid
    Assist you in deciding how to best pay for your Georgia State education and answer a variety of funding related questions.
  • Civic Engagement
    Focused on connecting students, staff, and faculty with meaningful community involvement.
  • University Career Services
    Offers and abundance of resource materials (i.e. books, periodicals and newsletters) in addition to computer workstations for internet based career research, resume writing, and personal assessments.
  • Writing Studio  
    Assist students with writing skills for course work, scholarship personal essays, personal statements and preparation for the Regent’s test.
  • University Housing
    Provides quality on-campus housing to enhance the personal growth and development of students.
  • Study Abroad
    Learn more about overseas study opportunities at Georgia State University and locating was to pay for study abroad costs. 
  • Testing Services
    Provides testing for ACT, CLEP, Math Placement test, GRE, GMAT, LSAT, PRAXIS, MAT, SAT, and more.
