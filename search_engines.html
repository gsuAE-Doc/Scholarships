Search Engines

This area includes links to internal and external scholarship search engines. Most scholarship search engines are managed by organizations which have no direct affiliation with the university, but may be able to offer additional financial support for our students.

 

Georgia State University Georgia State University scholarship search website is for currently enrolled students and provides additional resources.
Atlanta Scholarships Atlanta Scholarships is a source for local scholarship information. Greater Atlanta area high school students and Georgia based high school students can use this site.
College Answer This site is called CollegeAnswer and its six-part process aims to answer all your questions about paying for school.
College Board Students are familiar with CollegeBoard because of its association with the SAT, but the website also has a search that matches scholarships to your individual talents based on a questionnaire.
CollegeNet CollegeNET features a discussion forum where students can post about art, religion, politics, technology, and more. There is also a college and scholarship search.
College Scholarships College Scholarships is devoted to giving tips about how to get scholarships and even has simple assays. Students can apply to some scholarships directly from their website.
CollegeView As well as a scholarship matching engine, This site provides tips and helpful hints about application processes, as well as a calendar of upcoming college admissions chat sessions featured on the site.
Club Scholarship Club Scholarships offers a personal survey that will help you find the best college-funding recommendations for you.
Community Foundation The Community Foundation for Greater Atlanta has a collection of scholarship funds that allow donors to invest in the higher education of deserving students.
COS Funding The most comprehensive source of funding information available on the web, with more than 25,000 records, representing over 400,000 funding opportunities, worth over $33 billion.
Go College GoCollege has information on college admissions as well as a financial aid guide and college survival tips.
International Financial Aid Center IEFA is a resource for US and international students who wish to study abroad to find financial aid, college scholarships and grant information.
International Scholarships InternationalScholarships.com is a database for students of any origin. This site helps students find awards designed to assist those who wish to study in another country.
LittleAfrica LittleAfrica.com is a resource for African American students to search through hundreds of scholarships and internships.
Military.com This site helps students in the military community find scholarships and grants.
Scholarships.Fatomei This is a resource for scholarships separated into many different categories.
Scholarships 101 Scholarships 101 is a search engine with access to information about over 6.2 million scholarships and grants. Listings are updated daily.
Scholarships Scholarships.com is a search engine that students can connect with using facebook and twitter.
